GAME CONTROLS:
	*WASD to move around
	*Mouse to move camera
	*Hold right click to aim, let go to fire
	*1, 2, 3 to change difficulty (easy, normal, hard respectively)

DEBUG CONTROLS: (Press ` to activate)
	*Hold right click to move camera
	*WASD/EQ to move camera
	*Left click when brush is enabled to paint instanced renderers
